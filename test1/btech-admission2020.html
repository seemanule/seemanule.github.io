About MIT ADT University

Creativity and innovation co-exist and thrive at MIT-ADT University, Pune. Located at Rajbaug Campus, Loni Kalbhor, the award-winning campus spans over 100 acres of greenery and inspiration. The private University is established as per the Government and University Act and offers Undergraduate, Post-Graduate and Doctoral degrees in Art, Design, and Technology through industry-focused programs.

The MIT SOE (School of Engineering) prepares students to find solutions for all the technological and social challenges of the 21st century. Every student gains the skills and expertise to lead giant organizations in the corporate landscape. The University perfectly blends technical education with cultural values for all-round development.

Eligibility

1. The student must have passed (10 + 2) board Examination of State Board or CBSE or ICSE or IB with subjects group of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and/or Technical Vocational Subjects.
2. Open Category student should have scored minimum 50% Marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics with English as a subject.
3. Reserved Category student should have scored minimum 45% Marks Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics with English as a subject.
4. In addition to this, the applicant must have qualified at least one engineering entrance examination like MHT-CET 2020 /JEE (Mains 2020)/other states entrance exam of current year / Uni-GAUGE-E 2020.
5. Reserved Category students should fulfill the criteria of caste and caste validity certificate as per the norms of Govt. of Maharashtra.
Note: There is no age limit for admission.
